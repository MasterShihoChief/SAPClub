The BISSOP Program (Business Information Systems Student Outreach Program) was founded to help address the concerns between the College of Business Information Systems department and our industry partners. This program acts as a student outreach platform with the intention of educating and recruiting prospective students from local high schools and junior colleges into our major. Industry partners have expressed concerns over graduating student shortages and the next wave of entry-level positions that they need filled. The Information Systems field is growing exponentially and we aim to get ahead of the trend. Our second objective is to create a networking environment and open stronger lines of communications between students, mentors and counselors.
